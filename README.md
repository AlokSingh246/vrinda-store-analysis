# 📊 Vrinda Store Sales Data Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **Vrinda Store's 2022 sales data** using Microsoft Excel. The objective is to transform raw sales data into an interactive dashboard that provides valuable business insights regarding customer behavior, sales performance, product categories, order status, and sales channels.

The dashboard enables business owners to make data-driven decisions by identifying trends, top-performing regions, customer demographics, and sales patterns.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze annual sales performance.
- Identify top-performing states and sales channels.
- Compare sales by gender and age group.
- Analyze order status distribution.
- Identify monthly sales trends.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Build an interactive Excel dashboard using Pivot Tables and Charts.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

The interactive dashboard provides insights on:

- 📅 Monthly Sales vs Orders
- 👩 Men vs Women Sales Comparison
- 👥 Sales by Age Group
- 🌍 Top Performing States
- 🛍️ Sales by Product Category
- 🚚 Order Status Distribution
- 🛒 Sales by Online Channel (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Ajio, etc.)
- 🎛️ Interactive Slicers for dynamic filtering

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Women contribute significantly more sales than men.
- Adult customers generate the highest revenue.
- Delivered orders account for the majority of transactions.
- Amazon and Myntra are among the leading sales channels.
- A few states contribute a major share of total sales.
- Monthly sales trends help identify seasonal demand.
